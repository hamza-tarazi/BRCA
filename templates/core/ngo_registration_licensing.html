NGO Registration & Licensing

Facilitating NGOs with Registration and Licensing Services

Registering and obtaining licensing for Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) is a crucial step towards initiating impactful social work and community development projects. At BRCA Consultancy, we offer comprehensive services tailored to simplify the process, ensuring NGOs can focus on their core mission without bureaucratic hurdles.

BRCA Consultancy is majorly expert in registration of Non-Profit Organizations (NPO) or Non-Government Organizations (NGO). Majorly in Pakistan, Four (04) types of entities register NPOs/NGOs, which includes:
Security Exchange Commission of Pakistan (Section 42 License)
The Voluntary Social Welfare Agencies (VSWAs) Registration and Control Ordinance, 1961
Directorate of Social Welfare
Provincial Benevolent Fund Board

Navigating PCP Certification

Obtaining Professional Certification in Project Management (PCP) is a significant milestone for professionals seeking to enhance their project management skills and credibility. At BRCA Consultancy, we provide comprehensive support to individuals and organizations looking to pursue PCP certification, guiding them through every step of the process with expertise and efficiency.

  • Drafting application for certification from Pakistan Center for Philanthropy (PCP)
  • Drafting of Policies and Procedures as per the standards of Pakistan Center for Philanthropy (PCP)
  • Guidance to maintain records/files under the standards of Pakistan Center for Philanthropy (PCP)
  • Assist in replying to queries for evaluation of the organization during the certification process from Pakistan Center for Philanthropy (PCP)
  • Application for renewal of certification from Pakistan Center for Philanthropy (PCP)
